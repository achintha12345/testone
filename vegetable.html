How do we get our vegetable supply

Farmers

These are people who grow vegetables on their farms.They work hard to produce different kinds of veggies using different methods.

Farmers make sure that supermarkets have fresh and local vegetables to sell.

Vegetable Collectors

These are middlemen who gather vegetables from many farms. They buy veggies in large amounts and then sell them to supermarkets. Collectors help supermarkets get a lot of different vegetables from different places.

Regional Vegetable Collecting Centers

These places are like big storage areas for vegetables. They collect veggies from many farms in one area. These centers help organize and store vegetables before sending them to supermarkets. They make sure supermarkets always have enough fresh veggies.
